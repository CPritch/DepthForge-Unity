DepthForge — Third-Party Notices
================================

This package and the model it ships are built only on components that are clean
for commercial use. Components under non-commercial or research-only licenses are
NEVER shipped and never contribute training signal to the shipped model (they are
used, if at all, only as private visual reference of theoretical limits).
